Who We Are

From Simple Ideas to Beautiful Spaces

Maison Furniture Holders was founded in Arusha, Tanzania with a simple but powerful idea — that every home, no matter the size, deserves to be beautiful, comfortable and well designed.

We started with simple ideas and have grown into a trusted furniture and home decoration business. We deal in the selling of quality home furniture and home design decorations — carefully selected pieces that bring warmth, elegance and personality to your living spaces.

From a complete living room set to individual decoration pieces, our goal is always the same: to give you a home you love coming back to. We serve customers across Arusha and Tanzania, with 24/7 availability so you can reach us whenever you need us.
